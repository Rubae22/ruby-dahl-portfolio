Pholish’

Project

Brand Identity

Medium

Adobe Illustrator

Year

2023

This project was completed for a graphic design course, where we were tasked with creating a full brand identity for an original concept. The assignment required developing a logo suite, brand identity, posters, and various mockups, with each collateral piece relating directly to the nature of the brand.

I developed ‘Pholish’, a Vietnamese Tex/Mex fusion food truck that blends flavours from both cuisines to create unique and exciting dishes. The collateral was designed around the idea of items used to carry and serve food, reflecting the practical, on-the-go nature of a food truck. Although the physical items were not produced, the project focused on learning how to effectively present designs using mockups and branding applications.
