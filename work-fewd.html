Canberras Modern Re-Design

Project

Front End Web Design (UC)

Medium

Figma and VSC

Year

2026

This was a semester-long website redesign project for Canberra Modern, developed across research, design, and final build stages. The organisation promotes Canberra's mid-century modern architectural heritage through events and education, so the site needed to clearly support both event information and ongoing engagement.

The project involved auditing the existing website, developing a redesigned information structure, and producing a new visual direction grounded in mid-century modern design principles. This was translated into a working prototype and final website, focusing on clearer navigation, improved hierarchy, and more effective presentation of event and organisational content.

The work ran across three assignments over the full semester, moving from auditing and identifying issues, to prototyping, to the final site hand-coded in HTML and vanilla CSS. The prototypes and live build are embedded below.
