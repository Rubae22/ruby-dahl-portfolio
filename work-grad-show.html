Grad Show Logo

Project

Competition (ANU)

Medium

Adobe Illustrator

Year

2025

This project was completed for the ANU Grad Show Logo Competition 2025. The brief asked us to create a visual identity that reflected the diversity, creativity and energy of the graduating cohort, with a strong focus on typography.

For my submission, I chose to explore a 'type salad' approach, combining a mix of different typefaces to represent the individuality and eclectic nature of the graduating students. The result was a bold and expressive design that could adapt across a range of formats, including digital and print collateral.

Throughout the project, I focused on achieving a balance between visual impact and clarity. I carefully considered composition, contrast and hierarchy to ensure the design remained legible and cohesive, while still celebrating variety and experimentation.
