Type Animation

Project

Design for Digital Contexts (UC)

Medium

Adobe After Effects

Year

2026

This project was completed for a motion design course, where we were tasked with creating a 20–30 second kinetic typography animation showcasing a typeface of our choice. The assignment focused on using animation, hierarchy and pacing to create an engaging type specimen while maintaining readability and synchronising the motion with music.

For this project, I chose to feature Avant Garde, drawing inspiration from the typeface’s association with mid-century modern design. The animation combines geometric forms, a mid-century colour palette and rhythmic motion to reflect the typeface’s history and visual character, creating a dynamic digital type specimen that celebrates both its form and personality.
